# Use Case: Plan a Month of Social Media Content

## Overview

**Problem:** You need consistent social media presence but creating daily posts takes hours each week.

**Solution:** Use the `social-media` plugin to generate a complete 30-day social calendar in under 20 minutes.

**Time Saved:** ~10 hours per month (from 12 hours to 2 hours)

**ROI:** At $60/hour, that's $600 saved per month

---

## Who This Is For

- Social Media Managers
- Content Marketers
- Marketing Managers
- Founders/Solopreneurs
- Brand Managers

---

## What You'll Get

✅ 30 days of social posts (LinkedIn, Twitter, Facebook)  
✅ Mix of content types (educational, promotional, engagement)  
✅ Optimal posting times  
✅ Hashtag recommendations  
✅ Image/video suggestions  
✅ Engagement prompts

## Step-by-Step Instructions

### Step 1: Define Your Content Strategy (5 minutes)

Answer these questions:

**Platforms:**
- Which platforms? (LinkedIn, Twitter, Facebook, Instagram)
- How often per platform? (Daily, 3x/week, etc.)

**Content Mix:**
- 40% Educational (tips, how-tos, insights)
- 30% Thought leadership (opinions, trends)
- 20% Social proof (case studies, testimonials)
- 10% Promotional (product, offers)

**Themes:**
- What topics? (e.g., "sales automation", "AI in marketing")
- What keywords? (for discoverability)
- What tone? (professional, casual, humorous)

**Example Strategy:**
```
Platform: LinkedIn
Frequency: 5 posts/week (Mon-Fri)
Themes: Sales automation, AI prospecting, productivity tips
Tone: Professional but approachable, data-driven
Goal: Build thought leadership, generate leads
```

### Step 2: Generate Your Calendar (5 minutes)

1. Open Claude Code
2. Run:
   ```
   /social-media:plan-calendar
   ```
3. Describe your strategy:

**Example Prompt:**
```
Create a 30-day LinkedIn content calendar for a B2B SaaS company
selling sales automation tools.

Posting frequency: 5 posts/week (Monday-Friday)
Content themes: Sales automation, AI in sales, productivity, 
                sales leadership
Tone: Professional but conversational, data-driven
Mix: 40% tips/how-tos, 30% thought leadership, 20% case studies, 
     10% product
Goal: Build authority, generate demo requests
```

### Step 4: Customize Posts (30-45 minutes)

For each post:

**Add specifics:**
- Your company's data
- Real customer names (with permission)
- Actual statistics from your business
- Links to your content

**Personalize:**
- Add your voice and style
- Include relevant emojis
- Tag relevant people/companies
- Add your CTAs

**Optimize:**
- Check character limits
- Add hashtags (3-5 per post)
- Include images/videos
- Set posting times

### Step 5: Schedule in Your Tool (15 minutes)

**For Buffer/Hootsuite:**
1. Import posts to queue
2. Set posting schedule
3. Add images
4. Enable auto-posting

**For LinkedIn native:**
1. Create posts as drafts
2. Schedule each one
3. Add media
4. Set notifications

**For Later/Planoly:**
1. Upload content
2. Drag to calendar
3. Add captions
4. Schedule

## Hashtag Strategy

### How Many?
- **LinkedIn:** 3-5 hashtags
- **Twitter:** 1-2 hashtags
- **Instagram:** 10-15 hashtags
- **Facebook:** 1-2 hashtags

### Which Ones?

**Broad (High volume, low engagement):**
- #Sales
- #Marketing
- #Business

**Medium (Moderate volume, good engagement):**
- #SalesAutomation
- #B2BSales
- #SalesTech

**Niche (Low volume, high engagement):**
- #SDRLife
- #SalesProductivity
- #AIinSales

**Mix:** 1 broad + 2 medium + 2 niche

---

## Posting Schedule

### Best Times to Post

**LinkedIn:**
- Tuesday-Thursday
- 8-10 AM, 12-1 PM, 5-6 PM
- Avoid weekends

**Twitter:**
- Monday-Friday
- 9 AM, 12 PM, 3 PM, 6 PM
- High frequency (3-5x/day)

**Facebook:**
- Wednesday-Friday
- 1-3 PM
- Lower frequency (1x/day)

**Instagram:**
- Monday, Wednesday, Friday
- 11 AM, 2 PM, 7 PM
- Stories: Multiple times/day

---

## Measuring Success

### Key Metrics

**Engagement Rate:**
- Likes + Comments + Shares ÷ Followers
- Target: 2-5% on LinkedIn

**Reach:**
- How many people saw your post
- Track trends over time

**Click-Through Rate:**
- Clicks ÷ Impressions
- Target: 1-3%

**Follower Growth:**
- New followers per month
- Quality > quantity

**Lead Generation:**
- Demo requests from social
- Content downloads
- Website visits
