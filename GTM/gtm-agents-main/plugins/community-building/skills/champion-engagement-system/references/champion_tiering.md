# Champion Tiering Framework

## Tier 1: Strategic Advisors (Top 1%)
**Profile:** Industry thought leaders, C-level execs at strategic accounts.
**Commitment:** High impact, low frequency.
- **Activities:** CAB membership, press quotes, keynote speaking.
- **Benefits:** Direct access to product roadmap, VIP event invites, executive networking.
- **Support:** 1:1 White glove management by Exec Sponsor.

## Tier 2: Community Leaders (Top 10%)
**Profile:** Power users, community moderators, frequent contributors.
**Commitment:** High frequency, medium impact.
- **Activities:** Beta testing, forum moderation, user group leadership, blog posts.
- **Benefits:** Early access features, swag packs, certification vouchers, profile badge.
- **Support:** Dedicated Community Manager, private Slack channel.

## Tier 3: Advocates (Active Users)
**Profile:** Satisfied customers willing to share.
**Commitment:** Low frequency, low barrier.
- **Activities:** G2 reviews, social shares, referrals, survey responses.
- **Benefits:** Referral bonuses, digital badges, public shoutouts.
- **Support:** Automated program emails, self-serve portal.

## Movement Rules
- **Promotion:** Consistent contribution for 2 quarters + Nomination.
- **Demotion:** Inactivity for 2 quarters -> Move to Alumni status.
