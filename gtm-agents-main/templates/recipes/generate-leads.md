# Recipe: Generate 100 Qualified Leads

⏱️ **Time:** 2 minutes  
📊 **Difficulty:** Beginner  
🔌 **Plugin:** sales-prospecting

---

## What You'll Get
- 100 company names with details
- Contact information for decision-makers
- Personalized talking points
- Export-ready CSV file

---

## Quick Steps

1. **Install plugin** (one-time):
   ```
   /plugin install sales-prospecting
   ```

2. **Run command**:
   ```
   /sales-prospecting:generate-leads
   ```

3. **Describe your ideal customer**:
   ```
   "B2B SaaS companies, 50-200 employees, Series A funded, in the US"
   ```

4. **Export results** to your CRM

---

## Example Prompts

**For SaaS:**
```
Generate 100 leads for B2B SaaS companies with 50-200 employees,
Series A or B funded, located in the United States
```

**For E-commerce:**
```
100 e-commerce brands, $5M+ revenue, using Shopify Plus,
selling consumer goods in the US
```

**For Services:**
```
Professional services firms, 20-100 employees, focused on
digital transformation, located in major metro areas
```

---

## Pro Tips

✅ Be specific about industry, size, and location  
✅ Include 3-5 qualifying criteria  
✅ Start with 25 leads to test quality  
✅ Use buying signals like "recently raised funding"

---

## Next Steps

After generating leads:
1. Import to CRM
2. Build email sequence with `/email-marketing:design-campaign`
